Covid-19 Announcement

During these weird and trying times we will be continuing our coffee and waffle service, but doing everything to-go, in line with the current restrictions placed on bars and restaurants. We will be allowing ten people into the space at once to stand and wait for their orders, and we encourage customers to practice social distancing while inside the store. We've moved all our tables and chairs to provide more space.

We've set up merch tables for people to buy shirts and hoodies. Another way you can help us is to buy a gift card. Take out orders and gift card sales will allow us to have the funds to continue to pay our staff their full wages, especially if there is a forced shutdown, so no one loses their job and is forced to go on unemployment. These gift cards can be used for wine too when that gets back up and running. Thanks to everyone for your generous support and well wishes over the past couple of days.
